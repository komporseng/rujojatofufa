Kyla Rae Kowalewski is an American voice actress who is best known for being the voice behind the character of Anais Watterson and Rosie on The Amazing World of Gumball. Other than that, she has also voiced the character of various other The Powerpuff Girls, We Bare Bears, Adventure Time, and Ni Hao, Kai-Lan.

Kyla Rae Kowalewski Facts

  • She spent her childhood years in Chicago, Illinois.
  • Kyla and her family moved to California in order for her to hone in on her skills in the entertainment industry.
  • She made her debut as a voice actress on the show Ni Hao, Kai-Lan in 2009, where she voiced the character of FuFu.
  • In 2012, the vocals of her character of Anais Watterson was used in the video game called The Amazing World of Gumball: Mutant Fridge Mayhem.
  • She describes herself as a Californian girl.
  • Kyla was raised a catholic.
  • She was only 7 years old when she started her career as a professional voice actress.
  • Kyla admires the work of several singers and songwriters including Ed Sheeran, Halsey, Niall Horan, Charlie Puth, Kesha, Sam Smith, Demi Lovato, Liam Payne, and Logic.
  • She attended the 2017 KIIS-FM Jingle Ball.
