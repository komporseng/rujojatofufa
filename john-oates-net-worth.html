John Oates | Lifestyle and Vacation

Lifestyle

Multi-millionaire artist, musician, and producer John Oates lives a luxurious lifestyle with a net worth of $60 million.

He was born on April 7, 1948, in New York City but grew up in North Wales, Pennsylvania, a Philadelphia suburb.

Ann De Palma, his mother, was an Italian immigrant, and Al Oates, his father, was English and Gibraltarian.

John wrestled as a child, even captaining his high school wrestling squad and earning the local champion in his weight class.

His abilities earned him wrestling scholarships at several institutions, but he ultimately chose Temple University to reside in Philadelphia.

He decided to discontinue wrestling during his first year because of the constant necessity to make his weight category become exhausting.

In 1966, he released his first song, “I Need Your Love,” for Philadelphia-based Crimson Records.

Following his graduation from North Penn High School in 1966, John enrolled at Temple University in Philadelphia.

At the university, he met his bandmate and life-long friend, Daryl Hall, who was his senior at that time.

Although Oates and Hall were in separate bands at the time, they realized they liked the same type of music and decided to establish the duo “Hall & Oates.”

They shared a series of apartments and began composing music together, though they did not start working on recordings until 1970.

John has been married twice in his life. After dating for around five years, he first married model Nancy Hunter in 1983. They eventually divorced in 1988.

Later on, John began dating Aimee Oakes, with whom he later married in 1995.

The happy couple has been married for over 25 years and has a son named Tanner.

John Oates | Career

In 1972, “Hall & Oates” was signed to Atlantic Records.

The duo’s first several albums were not well received, so they left Atlantic Records in 1975 and signed with RCA Records, where they recorded “Daryl Hall & John Oates.”

The album included the singles “Sara Smile” and “She’s Gone,” both of which charted in the Top 10 on the Hot 100.

They recorded another album, “Bigger Than Both of Us,” the following year.

“Rich Girl,” the album’s second single, became the pair’s first number one smash.

Moreover, they have eleven number-one singles and over 20 top 40 records to their credit.

“1000 Miles of Life,” Oates’ second solo album, was released on August 23, 2008.
