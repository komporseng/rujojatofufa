Who Is Efrat Dor Husband? Know Sneaky Pete's Actress Love Life

The Zookeeper's wife actress Efrat Dor is married to her long-time husband Moshik Maymon. The Israeli actress does not flaunt her relationship publicly. She only posts about her professional life on social media.

Due to Dor's secretive nature, her dating life has been a mystery. Although the couple's initial meet and greet is unknown, Efrat and Moshik's wedding was in September 2010.

It has been more than a decade of their married life and the husband-wife duo, Dor, and Maymon have been successfully maintaining a low profile. They have not made any headlines with disputes and extramarital affairs.

However, some internet tabloids claim that Efrat got separated from her spouse in December 2020. Neither Dor nor Maymon has denied or accepted these rumors to date.
