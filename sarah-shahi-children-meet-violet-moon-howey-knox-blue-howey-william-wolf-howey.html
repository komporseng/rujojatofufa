Sarah Shahi children: Meet Violet Moon Howey, Knox Blue Howey, William Wolf Howey

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 22, 2024

42-year-old actress and former model Sarah Shahi hails from the United States.

She portrayed Carmen in the 2005 television series The L Word, Kate Reed in the 2011–2012 season of the USA Network legal drama Fairly Legal, and Sameen Shaw in the CBS crime thriller Person of Interest.

She appeared in the television show Reverie in 2018 and made a recurring appearance in City on a Hill on Showtime.

Sarah Shahi children: Meet Violet Moon Howey, Knox Blue Howey, William Wolf Howey

Shahi and her ex-husband, actor Steve Howey gave birth to their first child in July 2009. In March 2015 they were blessed with twins, a son, and another daughter.

Shahi is best known for her work on the television shows Alias, Dawson’s Creek, Reba, and Supernatural.

On The L Word in 2005, she played DJ Carmen de la Pica Morales in a supporting role. She joined the show in its second season. After the fourth season, Shahi’s two-year contract was not renewed, and her character was eliminated.
