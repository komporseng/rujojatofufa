Keisha Grant Bio | Wiki

Keisha Grant is an American journalist from West Hartford, Connecticut, United States. She currently serves as a TV News Anchor at WVIT (NBC Connecticut). Keisha has been with the channel for the last 18 years 9 months since Jan 2003. Her major role is to co-anchor the 6 and 11 pm newscasts.

Prior to joining her current network, Keisha worked as a Weekend Anchor/Reporter in Columbus, Ohio (WSYX/ABC6) owned by Sinclair Broadcast Group. She served the network for 3 years 2 months from Nov 1999 until Dec 2002. Before that, Keisha worked in Palm Springs, California at
KMIR 6 News. She served as the station’s General Assignment Reporter for 2 years 4 months from Jul 1997 until Oct 1999

Keisha Grant NBC | Ct

Currently, Keisha serves as an anchor for NBC Connecticut.
