Stewart Hagestad Wiki: Inside His Family &amp; Personal Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Stewart Hagestad is an American amateur golf player. Currently, he ranks world no.14 in the World Amateur Golf Ranking.

The player had his career-best amateur ranking of world no.7. He now ranks 1360 in the Official World Golf Ranking.

Hagestad won the 2016 U.S. Mid-Amateur to earn a spot at the 2017 Masters Tournament. Similarly, he won his second U.S. Mid-Amateur title in 2021.

The 6-foot-5-inch won the 2016 Metropolitan Amateur and competed in the U.S. Open in 2017, 2018, 2019, and 2022.

Stewart played collegiate golf at the University Of Southern California. Prior to that, he played high school golf at International Junior Golf Academy in Hilton Head Island, South Carolina.

Stewart Hagestad Parents: Inside His Family Life

Both his brothers were collegiate athletes. George was a member of the water polo team at Stanford University. Likewise, Richard played collegiate football at the University of Southern California.

Stewart grew up with his father, mother, and siblings in Newport Beach, California, United States.
