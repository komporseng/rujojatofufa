Meet Adam Kurowski, Lindsey Kurowski Brother! His Age, Job, Dating

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, May 13, 2024

Adam Kurowski is the brother of Magnolia Network’s Inn the Works host Lindsey Kurowski. The show has returned for season 2 in 2022 where Lindsey restores historic motels and inns across the country. The first season of the show premiered in early 2021 when she also purchased the Oak Knoll Lodge in Big Bear Lake, Calif., determined to renovate it and give it the life it needed.

In season 2, Lindsey headed to the East Coast where she renovates Springs Inn, in a 40-room historic motel in her hometown of Berkshires in Massachusetts where her family still lives. In an interview, Lindsey had confirmed that her family was working together to fix up the retro 40-room motel.

Lindsey explained to House Beautiful, “I called in some favors to my longest friends and my dad. You may also catch a glimpse of my brother rollerblading in an empty pool and my grandmother getting forklift-certified — the usual.”

“You’ll see a person slightly out of their comfort zone as the new motel is twice the size of the Oak Knoll Lodge,” she told the outlet.

Since we can expect the whole family to be involved in this season’s project let’s learn a few things ahead about them. One of the members of her family is her younger brother Adam. Keep reading to know more about her brother.

Inn The Works: Meet Adam Kurowski, Lindsey Kurowski Brother

Adam Kurowski is one of the four children born to her parents Michael Kurowski and Stacey Gilfillan. His parents are divorced (before 2008) and have already moved on with their respective partners. Michael, 63-years-old (as of March 2022), retired in June 2017 as a lineman after working the job for 35 years.

In the same month, according to Lindsey’s Instagram, he remarried his second wife.

Meanwhile, Stacey married back on 17 May 2008 to her husband Christopher “Gilly” Gilfillan. She is working at Eli Lilly and Company as an executive pharmaceutical sales representative since March 2018 in San Antonio, Texas. She has been associated with the company for over 15 years.

Stacey is currently 56 years old while her new husband is 66 as of March 2022 at the time of this article.

Besides Lindsey, Adam also has another sister named Kelsey Kurowski. She turned 32 years old in May 2021. She works as an accountant at Challenger Cables Sales.

Adam appears with his sister on Inn the Works. He can be seen helping out his sister on the show a few clips of which has been shared on Instagram. He also has a brother named Matt Kurowski who is a graduate of Southwick High School.

Adam Kurowski Age

In December 2021, Adam Kurowski turned 22 years old.

Adam Kurowski Job

Adam Kurowski’s definite job hasn’t been shared on his socials and also doesn’t have a LinkedIn. On his Instagram bio, he has hashtagged #KnottyBoyz so he may be an employee in Knotty Pine.

Knotty Pine is a design company based in southern California that creates physical displays for marketing and artistic purposes. The team has created projects for big-name companies like Nike, ClifBar, Adidas, Live Nation, NBC, United Airlines, and more. But, he hasn’t been listed in the team of Knotty Pine.

So, his precise job remains unknown.
