Jalen Ramsey height and weight Measurement in meters, feet, KG and Ibs

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 5, 2024

On October 24, 1994, Jalen Lattrel Ramsey, a cornerback for the Los Angeles Rams of the National Football League, was born. He attended Florida State University, and the Jacksonville Jaguars selected him as the fifth overall choice in the 2016 NFL Draft.

Lamont Ramsey, an emergency medical technician (EMT) and Jalen’s training coach, and Margie Ramsey welcomed Ramsey into the world on October 24, 1994, in Smyrna, Tennessee. Jamal, Jalen’s older brother, played quarterback at Middle Tennessee State University in Murfreesboro, Tennessee, where their father had previously played football, and participated in football, basketball, and track at Battle Ground Academy in Franklin, Tennessee.

In a nearby park, Jalen honed his football abilities by competing against much older youngsters. Although his family supported the Florida Gators, he grew up rooting for the Miami Hurricanes.

Height in meters and feet

Jalen Ramsey’s height is 6 ft 1 in (1.85 m).

Weight in KG and ibs

Jalen Ramsey’s weight is 208 lb (94 kg)
