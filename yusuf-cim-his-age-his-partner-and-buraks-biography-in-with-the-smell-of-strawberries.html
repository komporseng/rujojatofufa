YUSUF IM: HIS AGE, HIS PARTNER AND BURAKS BIOGRAPHY IN WITH THE SMELL OF STRAWBERRIES

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 1, 2024

One of the great Turkish bets of the summer season has come from Divinity, a medium where  ‘With the smell of strawberry’ has just been released recently . This production  tells the story of Asli, a 19-year-old girl who dreams of becoming a famous pastry chef. However, one day when she is on her way to deliver one of the cakes she has been ordered to, she is involved in an accident with a stranger, Burak (Yusuf Çim).

An encounter that, without seeing it coming, will change their lives for both of them. But behind this wonderful story is a Turkish actor who has become  one of the leading men of the moment , Yusuf Çim. We analyze the professional career and the most unknown side of the artist. You’ll be surprised!

YOUR PROFESSIONAL CAREER

Born on September 26, 1991 in Istanbul, Turkey, Yusuf Çim is a Turkish actor, singer, TV presenter, and model. His professional career kicked off in 2011, when he participated in the ‘Turkey’s Best Model’ contest. A first contact with the world of entertainment that led him to be part of large fashion catwalks and image of large advertising brands.

In 2013 , he ventured into the music industry and released his first EP, ‘Olsun Bi Kere’ . However, later, she decided to direct her career towards acting. His first role on the small screen came with the series ‘Ezra’, written and directed by Tayfun Güneyer, in which he played the role of police officer Mustafa. A fiction that took him a year later, in 2015, to give life to Burak in ‘With the smell of strawberries’.

In the same year, Yusuf Çim started his career as a TV presenter with the show ‘Piramit’ . He was the main presenter of the competition program in which Evrim Akın and Gökhan Yıkılkan were the main coaches. In addition, he also hosted Show TV’s 2016 New Year special ‘Sibel Can ile Yılbaşı Özel’.

In recent years, the artist has joined numerous projects for the small and big screen. All this added to the fact that he has participated in various charitable activities. With the help of the ‘Down Syndrome Association,’ he created a project titled ‘Don’t Forget This Year!’ to help children with Down syndrome.

HIS PERSONAL LIFE

On a personal level, Yusuf Çim shows himself to be a faithful follower of social networks. In fact, he has more than 4 million followers on his Instagram account, a platform where he also shares his closest and most intimate face. In addition, he has shown that, apart from acting and sports, he is a great fan of fashion and photography.

On the other hand, in the field of love, according to the newspaper ‘Eldía.es’, the actor had a sentimental relationship with fellow actress Demet Özdemir , whom he met in ‘Con olor a fresas’. A relationship that led them both to get engaged, but the change of residence of the actress and her growing popularity cut short the possible marriage of the interpreters. At present, it is unknown if Yusuf Çim has a new love story, since the actor is very reserved with his personal life
